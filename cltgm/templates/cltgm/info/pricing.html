Pricing

Introduction for New Players

$50/player

If you and a few friends would like to learn the basics of how to play then this is the session for you. The Intro session includes one 3 to 4 hour session using a Starter Set for Dungeons and Dragons. Current choices are the Standard 5e Starter and the Stranger Things themed Starter.

Characters are chosen from a list of pregenerated characters included with the Set. Players are provided with one set of dice and one painted miniature to use for the gaming session. The game can be played in the comfort of your own home, at a local game store, or at a public location of your choosing that is gamer friendly, such as a coffee bar, brewery, and some restaurants.

The fee is $50 per participating Player. The standard group is 4 to 6 Players.

Introduction for Families

$50/adult, $75/child

If you would like to introduce the magic you remember from childhood to your children or you just plain don’t understand what your kids keep talking about then this session is for you. The Intro for Families session includes one 3 to 4 hour session using a Starter Set for Dungeons and Dragons. Current choices are the Standard 5e Starter and the Stranger Things themed Starter. Each Starter will be edited based upon the age of your children to ensure family focused fun isn’t ruined by complicated rulesets or adult themes or situations. For example, a gaming session for 10 to 12 year old Players is typically shorter and monsters are defeated but not killed.

Characters are chosen from a list of pregenerated characters included with the Set. Players are provided with one set of dice and one painted miniature to use for the gaming session. The Intro for Families are typically played at home where younger Players can feel comfortable trying something new with parents, siblings, or any other relative or friends.

The fee is $75 per participating Child Player and $50 per participating Adult Player. The standard group is 3 to 6 Players.

Reintroduction for Returning Players

$50/player

If you have played other tabletop roleplaying games in the past or want to return to D&D this session is for you. The Reintroduction session includes one 3 to 4 hour session using a Starter Set for Dungeons and Dragons. Current choices are the Standard 5e Starter and the Stranger Things themed Starter.

Characters are generated using an abbreviated character creation method made by us here at “Business Name” designed to speed up the process by including common races, classes, and the typical powers used by the appropriate class. Additionally, there are a few questions that are designed to create camaraderie between Players from the beginning instead of that awkward “So you’re all in a tavern…” beginning.

Players are provided with one set of dice and one miniature to use for the sexes, races, and common classes, but as such we cannot guarantee that the miniature will be painted. The game can be played in the comfort of your own home, at a local game store, or at a public location of your choosing that is gamer friendly, such as a coffee bar, brewery, and some restaurants.

The fee is $50 per participating Player. The standard group is 4 to 6 created due to the additional time and resources necessary to create the characters.

Custom Setting or Campaign

$75-100+/player

If you’re familiar with D&D 5e or you’ve tried one of our introductory Starter Set and want to continue with the same characters, or you have always wanted to try one of the numerous named adventures in the D&D universe then this session is for you. Sessions are 3 to 4 hours but require more coordination with the DM.

Characters may be carried over from the Starter Set, allowing you to reach new levels of power previously unavailable. Or you could start with brand new characters, either using the abbreviated character creation method made by us here at “Business Name” or custom character creation using all the available rules in 5e.

Players are provided with one set of dice and one miniature to use for the gaming session. Players will select their miniature from an assortment of sexes, races, and common classes, but as such we cannot guarantee that the miniature will be painted. Players may also already have their own dice or miniature, which may be used with the game. The game can be played in the comfort of your own home, at a local game store, or at a public location of your choosing that is gamer friendly, such as a coffee bar, brewery, and some restaurants. Custom campaigns may want to schedule multiple sessions at a time to ensure all Players are available once per month, bimonthly, or at any other schedule best for the Players.

The fee is $75 or more per participating Player, based upon the amount of writing and preparation needed for the session. Using a published adventure is $75 and a custom adventure or campaign is $100 or more based upon complexity and how long the campaign has been running. The standard group is 4 to 6 Players. The abbreviated character creation method is $5 per character created due to the additional time and resources necessary to create the characters. The custom character creation method is $10 per character created due to the additional time and resources necessary to comb through all available tomes of knowledge for your character.

Dungeon Challenge

$25/player

Are you looking just to keep the good times rolling on a schedule that suits you? We are currently looking to begin Dungeon Challenge sessions that meet at regular intervals at public locations in Charlotte, NC. Delve into the secrets of the subterranean Underdark on Wednesdays. Investigate urban intrigue in Waterdeep on Fridays. Sessions are drop in and $25 per Player. Deposits are required to hold your place and are non-refundable. Dungeon Challenges occur as long as there is at least one Player available, although the ideal group is between 4 and 6 players.

Dice

varies

You may fall in love with a particular dice set that we use or have such great success with it that you wish to purchase it during or after your session. Dice are typically sold as a set which includes a d4, d6, d8, d10, d%, d12, and d20. Prices vary based upon factors such as color, speckling, composition (plastic or metal), and other factors.

Dice can also be purchased at many local gaming stores or online through gaming stores. We do encourage you to purchase your supplies from a local game store or online game retailer instead of Big Box Stores or Online Emporiums, as your local game store is your portal to new games, players, and friends.

Miniatures

varies

You may fall in love with a particular miniature that we use and want to keep it as a souvenir or for your own gaming sessions. Miniatures are typically sold individually and may or may not include a “base” for the figure. Prices vary upon composition (plastic or metal), complexity of the figure (number of parts or amount of assembly), and whether the miniature is pre-painted or hand painted.

Miniatures can also be purchased at many local gaming stores or online through gaming stores. We do encourage you to purchase your supplies from a local game store or online game retailer instead of Big Box Stores or Online Emporiums, as your local game store is your portal to new games, players, and friends.
